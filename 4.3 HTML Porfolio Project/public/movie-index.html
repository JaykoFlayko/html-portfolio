The Best Movies According to Jason Avnon

My top 3 movies of all-time

1. All About The Benjamins

Cool, comedic and action-filled movie starring Ice Cube.

2. Old Dads

This is a recently released movie from 2023 that is comedic and isn't afraid to offend people unlike most entertainment nowadays.

3. In Time

An exciting and nerve-racking Science Fiction Thriller movie from 2011 that makes the watcher think about the bigger picture of life, regardless if the movie is fiction.
